Privacy Policy

Straight talk.
No surprises.

Here's exactly what we collect, how it's stored, and what we do with it. No legal fog, no hidden clauses.

01
Data Collection
We collect only the essential data needed to make Arise function — things like your account credentials and in-app activity. We do not collect anything beyond what is required for the app to work and improve.
02
Storage via Firebase
Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, an industry-standard cloud platform. Firebase applies encryption in transit and at rest. We follow Firebase's recommended security practices and configure our database with access rules that limit exposure.
03
No Data Selling
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third-party advertisers, data brokers, or any other outside parties. Your data exists to make your experience work — nothing else.
04
Data Deletion
You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at contact.arise.app@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests promptly and in accordance with applicable law.
05
Policy Updates
If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll notify you through the app. The most current version is always available at this URL. Minor clarifications may be made without notice.

By continuing to use Arise, you acknowledge and agree to this privacy policy. Last updated 2026.
